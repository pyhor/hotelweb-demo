Fine Dining

AAALLURING is famous fine dining restaurant franchise partnered up with our hotel to serve in all six hotel branches. We offer mainly South Korean cuisine such as vegetables, seafood, meats and alike in the Korean Menu. For those with a wider international food taste, we offer a wide selection of international cuisine such as bread, cold cuts, pasta and alike in the International Menu. We highly base our menus on health benefits along with deliciousness, therefore we use nutrition labels and caloric values as our basis for creating the food for our customers.

Pool

The pool areas are spaces where our customers can confidently relax and broaden their horizons in terms of relationships by interacting with each other and engaging in conversations over the poolsides. Our swimming pools are divided into 2 separate zones: the Adult Swimming Zone and the Children Swimming Zone. In the Adult Swimming Zone, it is 6 feet deep while in the Children Swimming Zone, it is 3.5 feet deep. To ensure the safety of our customers, lifeguards are always on duty in case of unexpected incidents in pools.

Mini Bar

DAHLIA is a mini bar branch from our hotel serving various beverages. The mini bar is divided into 2 sections: the Alcoholic Section and the Non-Alcoholic Section, both placed opposite to each other. Customers are encouraged to socialize with each other and break the cultural barrier by crossing over to the opposite section as a symbol. As incentive, we have been sponsored by famous beverage brands and offer a 15% discount for said beverages following a schedule.

Parking

The parking area is divided into 4 floors for each hotel branch to accomodate parking of our customers' vehicles, depending on which floor our customers are designated to. Customers simply need to approach the Front Desk and request a parking space after making or claiming their reservation. The Front Desk management will give you a set of instructions and guide to be followed regarding your parking area. In addition, our hotel offers scheduled valet parking service as well. Parking costs ₩36301 per 24 hours.
